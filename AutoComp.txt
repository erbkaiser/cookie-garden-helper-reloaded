Automatic plot setting for seed complete and some fix

You turn "Auto plot" button on, wait, change setting, wait, and you will complete seeds!
Warning 1: Recommend to turn on with auto clicker or auto GC tool.
Total seed cost exceeds million CPS.

Warning 2: You should unlock JQB and Duketator by manual savescumming.
These steps take very long time.

全自動種子コンプ機能と修正

Auto plotボタンを押してしばらく待ち、設定を変更してまた待つだけで、種子をコンプできる…はずです。
注意：オートクリッカーか自動GCクリックと併用することをお勧めします
種子の総コストが100万CPSを超えるためです
実際に使う際は、JQBとDuketatorをリセマラで獲得することをお勧めします

First stage (weed, brown mold, cromsphore) setting
雑草とカビ2種を取得するまでの設定
 Avoid immortal = OFF
 New Seeds = ON
 Mature Seeds = ON
 Check CPS mature = OFF

 Remove weeds = OFF
 Clean Garden = OFF
 Dying plants = ON
 Check CPS dying = OFF

 Avoid buffs = ON
 Rotate soil = OFF (fixed to fertilizer)
 Check CPS plant = OFF
 auto plot = ON

Second stage (After unlock cromsphore) setting
雑草とカビ2種を取得した後の設定
 Avoid immortal = OFF
 New Seeds = ON
 Mature Seeds = ON -> OFF
 Check CPS mature = OFF

 Remove weeds = OFF -> ON
 Clean Garden = OFF -> ON
 Dying plants = ON
 Check CPS dying = OFF

 Avoid buffs = ON
 Rotate soil = OFF (fixed to fertilizer) -> ON (fert/wood)
 Check CPS plant = OFF
 auto plot = ON
